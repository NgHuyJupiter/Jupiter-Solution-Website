# Services Page Design Specifications - Overrides

This document defines page-specific design rules for the **Jupiter Solutions Services Page**.

---

## 1. Grid Composition
*   **Pattern Name:** Service Matrix & Delivery Rails
*   **Visual Guides:** Vertical timeline rail on the delivery workflow section, styled with thin borders and sequential index indicators.

---

## 2. Interactive Service Cards
*   **Left Guide Strip:** Service layout list includes a `3px` left brand border (`bg-brand-600`) that expands vertically from bottom-to-top (`scale-y-0` to `scale-y-100`) on hover.
*   **Chevron indicators:** Top-right chevrons translate `2px` right on active hover.

---

## 3. Cooperation Models
*   **Structure:** 4-column model cards (Consulting, Fixed-price, Dedicated Team, NOC SLA).
*   **Card Hovers:** Moderate elevate shift (`hover:-translate-y-0.5`) and border tint change to `border-brand-200`.
